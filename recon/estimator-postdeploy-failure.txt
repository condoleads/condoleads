                  email throws AND swallows.

    All catch blocks at lib/actions/leads.ts:329-337 + L379-387
    print console.warn/error but return {success: true} regardless.
    If a row IS being inserted but the operator only checks for
    email and sees none, they perceive "no lead, no email" even
